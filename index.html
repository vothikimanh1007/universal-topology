The 4 Epistemic Pillars

Regardless of cultural or linguistic boundaries, our AI text analysis reveals that all major contemplative traditions converge on four universal "Super-Nodes" of reality.

1. The Interface (Matter)

Not the ultimate reality, but the "Graphical User Interface" of the universe. It is transient and malleable.

Gnosticism: Hyle / The Material Realm
Buddhism: Rupa / Form (Five Skandhas)
Daoism: Wu / The Ten Thousand Things

2. The Observer (Mind)

The localized point of consciousness processing the interface. It is distinct from the material it observes.

Gnosticism: Nous / The Inner Man
Buddhism: Citta / Pure Awareness
Daoism: Xin / The Uncarved Block

3. The Loop (Ignorance)

The cognitive algorithms, biases, and fears that trap the Mind in a deterministic cycle of reactivity.

Gnosticism: Archons / The Seven Powers
Buddhism: Avidya / Samsara
Psychology: Cognitive Distortions / Priors

4. Root Access (Liberation)

The ultimate bypass. Disengaging from the Loop to directly apprehend and merge with Base Reality.

Gnosticism: Gnosis / Ascent of the Soul
Buddhism: Śūnyatā / Emptiness / Nirvana
Daoism: Wu-wei / Returning to the Root
